My Favorite Anime List

Attack on Titan

Humanity fights for survival against man-eating giants known as Titans. This anime is filled with intense action, deep storytelling, and emotional twists.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Tanjiro Kamado sets out on a journey to avenge his family and save his sister, battling powerful demons with breathtaking sword fights and visuals.

Death Note

A psychological thriller where a student discovers a notebook that allows him to kill anyone by writing their name. A battle of intellect unfolds between Light and L.
